Tabyldy Egemberdiev

Tabyldy Egemberdiev was a famous Entrepreneur from Kyrgyzstan. He had a big dream. He was going to promote kyrgyz traditions through business. He started with the Shoro company, which was going to produce natural drinks like maksym and chalap.

He also opened the cultural restraunt "Supara". There, he was going to create a place where people could enjoy local food, music and history. He believed culture and business could live together.

Egemberdiev wanted to inspire yougn people to make a business. He was going to invest in new ideas and help them to develop their own projects. His company was going to expand - not only in Bishkek but aslo in other regions and countries. He was sure, this was the way to success.

He believed that people should choose helthy food. "We are going to build our future and culture" - he said. He also warned: "don't always borrow from others - try to create your own values".

Thanks to this vision, many people began to think differently about tradition and business. His dream was that the land of Kyrgyzstan was always going to be proud of its culture.

    My future plans

    My name is Alex, and I am not just looking for a job - I want to build a good carreer. I have a clear plan, and I am going to make it happen. My goal is to become a successful entrepreneur in the food industry.

    First, I am going to invest a lot of time in learning. I am going to choose a university program that focuses on business and agriculture. This knowladge is fundamental I need. After graduation, I am going to work for a few years in a large food company to get experience, but my real dream is - to start my own business.

    My big dream is to create a company that makes healthy snacks. I am going to produce that snacks using only natural ingredients. So, I am going to build strong relationships with local farmers.

    Of course, starting a business is costs money. To reach my dream I am going to borrow a small amount of money from the bank. I will also use my own savings.

    Once the product is perfect, I am going to develop a strong brand, then create friendly web-site. Then the most exciting part begins: I am going to promote my products using all social media.

    My plan for the next five years is - to buy a small piece of land out of he city. I will build my own factory.

    I know the path to success is not easy. It takes hard work and the ability to learn from mistakes. But I have a clear vision, and I am going to follow my dream step by step.
