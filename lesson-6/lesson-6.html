From Bishkek to the world!

People always ask me, "Adilet, how do you do it? How do you travel around the world like this?" Well, it starts with a decition. A few years ago, I looked at a map and thought - "I am going to explore all of that". Sometimes I buy a one way ticket when I want freedom, and sometimes I choose a round way ticket when I know my schedule exactly.

I usually book my flight and hotel in advance. You have to check in early, then wait, and watch the departure time. My flight to Vienna is going to take off at 7:10 a.m. local time. I was always excited at that moment. After a long flight, we land on time and a new advanture begins.

In Vienna, I arrived late at my hotel. The first thing I do after I check in is to ask - "Where is the best place to exchange money?" I need local currency for the real adventure.

Ah! Vienna is a dream place for sightseeing. On my first day, I am going to explore local sightseeings after breakfast at 8:00 a.m. I decided to try local cuisine for lunch. I found a cozy cafe in a narrow street where I bought local food and wine.

My trip to Vienna is just one chapter. Every journey teaches me something new about the world.

For me, traveling is independance and confidance. Every hotel, every airport and every city gives me a new story. So, I am not just visiting places - I am living that moment.

Heads and Tails: Kyrgyzstan

Our new trip begins early in the morning at a Ukranian airport. Today, Orel i Reshka is going to travel to a mountain country - Kyrgyzstan. We arrive at the airport before sunrise, check our documents and prepare our luggage. The departure time is at 6:45 a.m. so everything must be on time.

One of us is going to travel with a one way ticket, a small backback and limited budget. The other has a round way ticket and much more comfort. At exacly 6:45 our plane starts take off and we know this trip will be full of surprises.

Our arrival time is 4:20 a.m. at local time. The plane slowly lands in Bishkek, International Manas Airport. The first thing we notice is the view of beautiful mauntains around the city.

At the airport, we exchange our money because we need local currency. After checking in at the hotel, we drop our luggage and prepare for our first day of sightseeing.

We are going to explore Kyrgyzstan through its culture and people. We will try traditional local cuisine, visit local markets and talk to local citizens. While walking around the city, we almost get lost, but that only helps us discover hidden streets and small cafes.

Later, when it's time to check out, we feel that this country has something special. This journey from Ukraine to Kyrgyzstan is not just travel - it's a story we will remember for a long time.
